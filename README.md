# BriefAI Backend

BriefAI is an AI-powered marketing platform built for Indian small business owners who have little or no marketing knowledge.

The user describes their product in simple language, and BriefAI automatically:

- Understands the business
- Creates a marketing strategy
- Generates WhatsApp marketing messages
- Generates Instagram captions
- Creates AI-powered product images
- Prepares content for direct Instagram publishing

The goal is to help small businesses create professional marketing content without hiring agencies, freelancers, or learning marketing concepts.

## Problem

Many Indian small business owners sell through WhatsApp and Instagram but struggle with:

- Writing effective marketing copy
- Creating attractive product creatives
- Understanding marketing terminology
- Affording marketing agencies

BriefAI solves this by turning simple business information into ready-to-use marketing content.

## Core Features

- Multi-step LangGraph AI workflow
- WhatsApp copy generation
- Instagram caption generation
- AI image generation using Flux
- Campaign quality checking
- Campaign history storage
- Instagram publishing workflow
- Multi-language support

## Tech Stack

- FastAPI
- LangGraph
- Mistral AI
- Flux (Replicate)
- Supabase
- Instagram Graph API
